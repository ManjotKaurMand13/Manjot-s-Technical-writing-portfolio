SHIV KUMAR BATALVI - MAH! FAVOURITE WRITER

Shiv Kumar Batalviwas an Indian poet, writer and playwright of the Punjabi language who was born on 23 july, 1936 and died on 6 may, 1973. He was most known for his romantic poetry. He became the youngest recipient of the Sahitya Akademi Award in 1967, given by the India's National Academy of Letters, for his epic verse play based on the ancient legend of Puran Bhagat, Loona (1965),now considered a masterpiece in modern Punjabi literature Today, his poetry stands in equalall of modern Punjabi poetry, like Mohan Singh (poet) and Amrita Pritam.[10]

His Life

Shiv was born on July 23, in a village Bara Pind Lohtian, In northen punjab before the the Indo-Pak partition. He is belonged to middle-class family who lives in this village from long generations. His Father Krishan Gopal was an Pandit(priest) and his mother Shanti Devi was an amazing melodious Singer. He studied at boys The Indo-Pak separation really affected his mind and the memories ofn his village that he had to left due to separation lead him to writings.
